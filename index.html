üìä How to Interpret Your Score:
< 3 months Insufficient coverage - vulnerable to financial shocks
3-6 months Adequate coverage - you're well prepared!

Solvency Ratio

Financial Stability Measure

0.0

üèõÔ∏è Understanding Solvency Ratio

What it measures: The proportion of your assets that you truly own (after paying off all debts). A measure of your long-term financial stability and ability to meet long-term obligations.

üìä How to Interpret Your Score:
< 10% You owe significantly more than you own - financial stability is at risk
10%‚Äì20% You have some equity but debts may pose long-term challenges
>20% You own the majority of your assets and have strong financial foundations
üî¢ What's Included:
Numerator: Net Worth (Total Assets - Total Liabilities)
Denominator: Total Assets
Formula: (Net Worth √∑ Total Assets) √ó 100%
üí° Improving Solvency Ratio:
  • Pay down outstanding debts to reduce liabilities
  • Increase savings and asset accumulation
  • Focus on building equity in long-term investments
  • Avoid taking on excessive debt relative to your assets
